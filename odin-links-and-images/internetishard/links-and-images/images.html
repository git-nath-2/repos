Images

This page covers common image formates, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras

JPGS

JPGs are good for photo they support different colors without extra size increaments

GIFs

GIFs are good for animation they don't support different colors and in transparent backgound lacks detail views

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons

SVGs

SVGs are amazing use them whereever you can they can be scaled up without loosing their quaility
